VLLM RELEASE

vLLM v0.20.2 ships hang and KV fixes

v0.20.2 lands a DeepSeek V4 MTP=1 hang fix, a KV-block allocation fix, and a gpt-oss MXFP4 torch.compile fix. Anyone running v0.20.x on these models should pin to .2.

vLLM v0.20.2 release Pin clusters running DeepSeek V4 or gpt-oss to v0.20.2 to avoid silent hangs.

PAPER

Irminsul: MLA-native position-independent caching

New arXiv preprint quantifies 10–16s TTFT spikes when bit-identical tokens shift position turn-over-turn, and proposes a closed-form correction. Useful primitive for reasoning about cache-hit invariants.

arXiv 2605.05696 20-min skim — clean precondition primitive for any work on action-boundary contracts.
